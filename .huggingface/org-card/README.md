# 🌐 CommunityOne

**The open path to everything local**

CommunityOne provides open-source tools and data for civic engagement, local government transparency, and nonprofit discovery across the United States.

## 🚀 Featured Projects

### [Open Navigator for Engagement](https://www.communityone.com)
A comprehensive platform for discovering, analyzing, and tracking:
- **90,000+ jurisdictions** (cities, counties, states)
- **3.9M nonprofit organizations** with financial data
- **153K+ public meetings** and agendas
- Local government officials and contact information

**Live App:** https://www.communityone.com

## 📊 Open Datasets

We publish high-quality, regularly-updated civic data:

- [**Nonprofit Organizations**](https://huggingface.co/datasets/CommunityOne/one-nonprofits-organizations) - 3.9M organizations with mission, location, NTEE codes
- [**Nonprofit Financials**](https://huggingface.co/datasets/CommunityOne/one-nonprofits-financials) - Revenue, assets, income data
- [**Nonprofit Programs**](https://huggingface.co/datasets/CommunityOne/one-nonprofits-programs) - Program descriptions and service areas
- [**Nonprofit Locations**](https://huggingface.co/datasets/CommunityOne/one-nonprofits-locations) - Geographic coordinates for 1.9M locations
- [**Public Meetings Calendar**](https://huggingface.co/datasets/CommunityOne/one-meetings-calendar) - 153K+ meeting records

**Total:** 13.8M records

## 🎯 Mission

Making civic data accessible, usable, and actionable for:
- Policy makers and advocates
- Researchers and journalists
- Nonprofit organizations
- Engaged citizens
